history

The first night in survival games is always tense. But here it is completely unclear what to do. The inventory contains a dozen blocks of dirt and a couple of pieces of raw meat from a killed cow. The sun has finally set, and visibility is zero. Somewhere from the darkness you can hear a roar and the clanging of bones. Strange mobs come out to meet you: zombies and a skeleton. The first one pushes you, and the second one shoots with a bow. HP tends to zero. The short battle ends with a strange hissing from behind and a loud explosion.
